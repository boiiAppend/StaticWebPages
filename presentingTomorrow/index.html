Types of Books available

Fiction Novels

A fictional novel is a literary work that presents an imaginative and invented narrative, often involving characters, events, and settings created by the author.

Horror Novel

A horror novel is a genre that elicits fear and terror through supernatural or psychological elements, creating an unsettling atmosphere.

Romance Novels

A romance novel is a literary genre that focuses on the emotional and romantic relationships between characters, typically centered around the theme of love.
